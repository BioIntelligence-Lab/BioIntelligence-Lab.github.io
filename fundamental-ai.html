Fundamental AI Research

Advancing the foundations of artificial intelligence to understand complex biological systems.

Overview

The Fundamental AI vertical develops core algorithms that enable new forms of scientific discovery in medicine and biology. Our work spans lifelong learning (ShELL), counterfactual modeling, AI safety and trustworthiness, and the development of autonomous agentic systems capable of performing scientific reasoning tasks.

These research programs drive both algorithmic innovation and biological insight, forming the foundation upon which our biomedical discoveries and computational tools are built.
