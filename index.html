BioIntelligence Lab

Understanding biological systems with Artificial Intelligence.

We develop AI methods and imaging-based models to reveal hidden structure in disease and population health. Our work spans foundational AI, computational biomedical discovery, and the informatics tools that make these advances usable in practice.
